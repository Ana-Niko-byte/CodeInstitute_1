Rotting Roots

This is a very common problem, usually caused by overwatering, poor soil, or soil fungi.

Brown Spots

This is usually cause by overwatering, underwatering, and lack of humidity. Strange, right? Another cause can be direct sunlight

Dying Leaves

This is a common problem, usually caused by cold air, overwatering, or dry air.

Thrip Infestation

Thrips are tiny black insects that feed on your plants and leave them looking dull, dry, and dreary.

White Mold

This is typically caused by high humidity and low airflow, causing the plant to essentially 'rot'.

Wilting Plant

This problem is usually caused by drought, or waterlogged soil.
